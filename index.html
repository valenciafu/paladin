Autonomous UAV Surveillance Platform

We want to
change the way
first responders operate.

We make an UAV fleet control software that allows responders to survey a scene before they arrive, cutting response time by at least 30 seconds.

Type in an address, hit launch, and the drone does the rest: from optimizing viewing angles, to analyzing the scene with advanced CV algorithms.

With a fire doubling in size every 30 seconds, every second is vital and can be the difference between life and death.

Our platform is for any private or public agency that needs instant surveillance and birds-eye-view of a situation, with an emphasis on public safety departments.
